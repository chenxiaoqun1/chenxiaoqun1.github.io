Chen Xiaoqun

Chen Xiaoqun received a B.S. in electronic information engineering, and a PhD. in electromagnetic field and microwave technology from Xidian University in 2005 and 2009.
He is an executive secretary of IEEE RFID Xi'an Chapter since 2020.
His research interests include system design of communication satellite and advanced communication satellite payload technology.
He works at Institute of Telecommunication and Navigation Satellites, China Academy of Space Technology.
